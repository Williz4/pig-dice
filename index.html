Welcome to Pig Dice!

"Pig Dice is a simple dice game played between two people. Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls a die until either a 1 is rolled (which makes the player lose their score that turn) or the player decides to "hold" (which allows a player to keep their turn score but ends their turn). The first player to score 100 or more points wins." For more information about this game and its different variations feel free to read up the following ARTICLE about it.
